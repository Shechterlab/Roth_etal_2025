#Prep Histone gene and snRNA gene gtf and bed files
=============

*ENSEMBL gtf to histone_genes_hg38.gtf*

`zcat ~/data/NGS/stds/refgenie/alias/hg38/gencode_gtf/default/hg38.gtf.gz | awk '$3 == "gene" && /HIST/ {print}' > histone_genes_gencode-hg38.gtf`
`zcat ~/data/NGS/stds/refgenie/alias/hg38/gencode_gtf/default/hg38.gtf.gz | awk '$3 == "gene" && /snRNA/ {print}' > snRNA_genes_hg38.gtf`


Make sure to include GTF header for gtf2bed (bedops)

e.g.
*description: evidence-based annotation of the human genome (GRCh38), version 32 (Ensembl 98)
*provider: GENCODE
*contact: gencode-help@ebi.ac.uk
*format: gtf
*date: 2019-09-05

Make sure that it is a tsv (tabs) not spaces)
